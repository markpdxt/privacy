TrafficScope Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

Overview

TrafficScope is an aircraft and marine vessel tracking app developed by PDXT, LLC. We respect your privacy. This policy describes what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is shared with third parties.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to PDXT, LLC. TrafficScope contains no analytics, no advertising, no telemetry, no crash reporting, and no user accounts.

Data Used on Your Device

The following data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to PDXT, LLC:

  • Location (When In Use / Always): Your device coordinates are used to query for nearby aircraft and vessels and to display your position on the map. If you enable background proximity alerts, the app requests "Always" location permission to continue monitoring traffic while backgrounded. Location data is not stored persistently.
  • API Credentials: If you use the RapidAPI aircraft data source or AISStream.io marine data source, your API keys are stored securely in the device keychain. They are never transmitted to PDXT, LLC.
  • App Preferences: Settings such as your selected data sources, filter criteria, proximity alert configuration, map zoom level, and API usage counters are stored locally on your device.
  • Aircraft Cache: Recently fetched aircraft data is temporarily cached in memory (30 seconds) and on disk (5 minutes) to reduce network requests. Cached data is automatically deleted when it expires.

Third-Party Services

Aircraft Data Sources

TrafficScope connects to one of three aircraft data sources, chosen by you:

  • RapidAPI (ADS-B Exchange): If you select the RapidAPI data source, your device's approximate latitude and longitude are sent to ADS-B Exchange via RapidAPI (adsbexchange-com1.p.rapidapi.com) to retrieve nearby aircraft positions. Your RapidAPI key is included for authentication. This communication is subject to RapidAPI's Privacy Policy and ADS-B Exchange's terms. No other personal data is transmitted in these requests.
  • ADSB.lol: If you select the ADSB.lol data source, your device's approximate latitude and longitude are sent to the ADSB.lol API (api.adsb.lol) to retrieve nearby aircraft positions. No API key or authentication is required. No personal data beyond your approximate coordinates is transmitted. This communication is subject to ADSB.lol's terms.
  • Local ADS-B Receiver: If you select a local receiver, TrafficScope connects directly to a URL on your local network that you configure. No data leaves your local network and no third-party services are contacted.

Marine Vessel Data Sources

TrafficScope connects to one of two marine vessel data sources, chosen by you:

  • AISStream.io: If you select the AISStream.io data source, your device's approximate geographic bounding box is sent via WebSocket to AISStream.io (stream.aisstream.io) to receive real-time vessel positions. Your AISStream API key is included for authentication. No other personal data is transmitted. This communication is subject to AISStream.io's terms.
  • Local AIS Receiver: If you select a local AIS receiver, TrafficScope connects directly to a URL on your local network that you configure. No data leaves your local network and no third-party services are contacted.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer any user data to third parties for advertising, marketing, or any other purpose. The only network communication is between your device and the aircraft or marine data sources you configure.

Data Retention

TrafficScope does not store any data on external servers. All data resides on your device:

  • Memory cache: cleared after 30 seconds
  • Disk cache: cleared after 5 minutes
  • Preferences and settings: persist until you delete the app
  • API keys: stored in the device keychain until you delete the app

Your Rights

Since all data remains on your device, you have full control:

  • Revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings
  • Delete all stored data by deleting the app
  • Clear the aircraft cache from within the app

Children's Privacy

TrafficScope does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
