BorderCast Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

Overview

BorderCast is a border crossing wait time app developed by PDXT, LLC. We respect your privacy. This policy describes what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is shared with third parties.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to PDXT, LLC. BorderCast contains no advertising and no user accounts.

Analytics

BorderCast uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve the experience. Firebase Analytics may collect:

  • Usage data: App opens, screen views, and feature usage (e.g., which crossings are viewed most)
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Crash data: Crash logs and performance metrics to help us fix issues

This data is anonymous and aggregated. It does not include your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information. Firebase Analytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Used on Your Device

The following data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to PDXT, LLC:

  • App Preferences: Settings such as your selected auto-refresh interval and favorite crossings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS preferences. They are never transmitted externally.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer any user data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Network communication is limited to:

  • The BorderCast API to retrieve publicly available border wait times
  • Google Firebase for anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting

Data Retention

BorderCast does not store any personal data on external servers. All personal data resides on your device:

  • Crossing data: refreshed on each fetch, not persisted
  • Preferences and favorites: persist until you delete the app
  • Analytics data: retained by Google Firebase per their data retention policies

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete all stored data by deleting the app
  • Change your auto-refresh interval at any time in Settings

Children's Privacy

BorderCast does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

Data Attribution

Border crossing wait time data is sourced from US Customs & Border Protection, the Washington State Department of Transportation, and the Canada Border Services Agency. This data is publicly available and provided by the respective government agencies.
