MAVLink Companion Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

Overview

MAVLink Companion is a drone telemetry companion app developed by PDXT, LLC. It connects to drones via the MAVLink protocol to display real-time flight telemetry, health status, and position data. We respect your privacy. This policy describes what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is shared with third parties.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to PDXT, LLC. MAVLink Companion contains no advertising and no user accounts.

Analytics

MAVLink Companion uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve the experience. Firebase Analytics may collect:

  • Usage data: App opens, screen views, and feature usage
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Crash data: Crash logs and performance metrics to help us fix issues

This data is anonymous and aggregated. It does not include your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information. Firebase Analytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Drone Telemetry Data

MAVLink Companion receives real-time telemetry data from your drone over a local UDP connection, including:

  • Flight data: Altitude, velocity, GPS coordinates, and heading
  • Health status: Battery level, satellite count, sensor calibration status, and arming state
  • Connection data: MAVLink heartbeat and connection state

All telemetry data is processed locally on your device in real time. It is never transmitted to PDXT, LLC or any external server. Telemetry data is not persisted after the app is closed.

Location Data

MAVLink Companion may request access to your device's location to center the map on your position when no drone is connected. This location data is:

  • Used only for the in-app map display
  • Never transmitted to PDXT, LLC or any external server
  • Not stored or logged

Data Used on Your Device

The following data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to PDXT, LLC:

  • App Preferences: Settings such as your configured MAVLink UDP port are stored locally on your device using standard iOS preferences. They are never transmitted externally.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer any user data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Network communication is limited to:

  • Local UDP connections to your drone for MAVLink telemetry
  • Google Firebase for anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting

Data Retention

MAVLink Companion does not store any personal data on external servers. All data resides on your device:

  • Telemetry data: displayed in real time, not persisted
  • Preferences: persist until you delete the app
  • Analytics data: retained by Google Firebase per their data retention policies

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete all stored data by deleting the app
  • Change your MAVLink port configuration at any time in Settings
  • Revoke location access in iOS Settings at any time
  • Limit analytics collection by enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" in your iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

MAVLink Companion does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
