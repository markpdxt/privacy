BorderCast Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

Overview

BorderCast is a border crossing wait time app developed by PDXT, LLC. We respect your privacy. This policy describes what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is shared with third parties.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to PDXT, LLC. BorderCast contains no analytics, no advertising, no telemetry, no crash reporting, and no user accounts.

Data Used on Your Device

The following data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to PDXT, LLC:

  • App Preferences: Settings such as your selected auto-refresh interval and favorite crossings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS preferences. They are never transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

BorderCast connects to a single data source to retrieve border crossing wait times:

BorderCast API (Cloudflare Worker): The app fetches current border crossing wait times from a PDXT-operated API hosted on Cloudflare Workers. This API aggregates publicly available data from US Customs & Border Protection and the Washington State Department of Transportation. No personal data, device identifiers, or location information is included in these requests.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer any user data to third parties for advertising, marketing, or any other purpose. The only network communication is between your device and the BorderCast API to retrieve publicly available border wait times.

Data Retention

BorderCast does not store any data on external servers. All data resides on your device:

  • Crossing data: refreshed on each fetch, not persisted
  • Preferences and favorites: persist until you delete the app

Your Rights

Since all data remains on your device, you have full control:

  • Delete all stored data by deleting the app
  • Change your auto-refresh interval at any time in Settings

Children's Privacy

BorderCast does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
